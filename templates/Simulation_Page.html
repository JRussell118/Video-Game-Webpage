What is a Simulation Game?

Simulation games are one genre of Video Games that focus on recreating an event, action, and/or behaviors that you can do in real life. Simulation games can give the player the experience of doing an action or behavior without having to use real life resources to perform the same actions in a less stressful environment. Some simulator games include farming, building and managing a town, or even living a different life entirely. Simulation games can replicate these actions as realisitcally as possible, or add a fictional aspect to the game to make it more unique than just doing the action in real life. Though some simulation games have become more common and mainstream with common behaviors like streaming, driving a truck, and even cutting grass.
