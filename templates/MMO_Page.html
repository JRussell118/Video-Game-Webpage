What is a MMO?

MMO games are one genre of Video Games that focus on online play and communication with millons of players around the world. A common aspect of these games is that they are mostly free to start with in game purchases to contribute to the games purchases with the amount of players it supports. This genre's main appeal is the ability to bring together millions of players and allow them to communicate and play together.

This genre's type of gameplay can vary from:

  1. Turn-based combat
  2. Real-Time combat
  3. Strategy
  4. Simulation
