What are Video Games?

Video games are a form of entertainment media that many people like to enjoy. They are unique from other media types in that they give a player an interactive role in a fictional environment. They also allow players to act out their own personal choices and actions with a unique, fictional experience depending on the genre of game. Video games have many genres can belong to which can affect the way the game is played and presented to the player.

Some Genres of Video Games

  • RPG (Role-Playing Games)

  • FPS (First Person Shooters)

  • Indie

  • Simulation

  • Adventure

  • Action

  • Puzzle

  • MMO (Massively Multiplayer Online)

  • Strategy

  • Fighting
